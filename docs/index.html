Bradley's Finance Hub

Velocity Banking & Financial Management Tool

Bradley's Finance Hub is a comprehensive iOS application designed to help you manage your finances, track debts, set budgets, and achieve your financial goals using velocity banking principles.

Key Features

  • Debt Tracking: Monitor and manage all your debts in one place
  • Budget Management: Create and track monthly budgets
  • Savings Goals: Set and achieve your savings targets
  • Transaction Tracking: Record and categorize all your financial transactions
  • Net Worth Calculator: Track your overall financial health
  • Velocity Banking: Optimize your debt payoff strategy
  • Zero-Based Budgeting: Plan every dollar of your income
  • Financial Reports: Generate detailed reports and insights

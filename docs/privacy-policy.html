Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Bradley's Finance Hub collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Financial data including debts, budgets, savings goals, transactions, and net worth
  • Payment history and contribution records
  • Activity logs of your financial actions

All data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. We do not collect, transmit, or share your financial information with any third parties.

2. Data Storage

Your financial data is stored securely on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. If you enable iCloud Sync (CloudKit), your data will be synchronized across your devices using your Apple ID. This synchronization is encrypted and managed by Apple.

3. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. All data is stored locally on your device with iOS security measures. If you use CloudKit sync, data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple.

4. Third-Party Services

Bradley's Finance Hub does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely using iOS native frameworks.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Export your data at any time using the Settings > Export Data feature
  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Disable iCloud Sync to stop cloud synchronization

6. Children's Privacy

Bradley's Finance Hub is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the 'Last Updated' date at the top of this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app support channels or visit our GitHub repository at https://github.com/ronb12/Bradleys-Financial-Hub.
